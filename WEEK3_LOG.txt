FINDING: NO2-shape CNN detects plants at 95% recall but confuses
concentrated industrial/urban hotspots with power plants.
- hard-only accuracy: 77.1%  (vs 91.2% easy task in Week 2)
- mixed accuracy: 87.5%
- worst false alarms: Jamshedpur, Bhilai (steel), Bangalore, Mumbai
- highways rarely confused (diffuse/linear, not point-like)
- Grad-CAM confirms model fixates on the compact NO2 hotspot

CONCLUSION: detector = "concentrated combustion detector", not
"power-plant-specific". Motivates multi-pollutant / spectral features.
